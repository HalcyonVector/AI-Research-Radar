The Big Story

Mixture-of-experts routing breaks out of the lab.

A wave of open implementations pushed sparse routing from research curiosity to production default this week — while three unfamous papers quietly began to matter.

Sparse expert routing dominated new GitHub implementations this week, with Scaling Language Models with MoE Routing leading on both citation velocity and momentum. The technique’s path from a single 2017 paper to commercial inference APIs now spans five organizations.

Elsewhere, agentic workflows continued to displace pure chain-of-thought reasoning. Self-Improving Agents via Verified Tool Use gained eight implementations in seven days, and tool-use benchmarks increasingly favor multi-step agents on real-world tasks.
